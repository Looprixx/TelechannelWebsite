Welcome To TeleChannel

Connecting Kohat Since 1999 Bringing entertainment and connection to the heart of Kohat, Telechannel has been a trusted provider of cable TV and internet services for over two decades. From the early days of analog cable to the cutting-edge offerings of digital cable and IPTV (coming soon), Telechannel has always strived to stay ahead of the curve, ensuring Kohat residents have access to the latest and greatest in entertainment and communication technology. Our mission is simple: to bridge the digital divide and keep the south region connected. We believe everyone deserves access to high-quality information and entertainment, regardless of location

What sets us apart? Deep roots in Kohat!, We understand the unique needs and preferences of the community

We offer reliable and affordable services, backed by exceptional customer support.
